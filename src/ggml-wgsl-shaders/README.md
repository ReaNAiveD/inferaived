# WGSL Shaders

The shaders are copied from [LLaMA.cpp](https://github.com/ggml-org/llama.cpp) and modified to work with our data layout and quantization scheme. They are used for matrix multiplication and other operations on the GPU. The shaders are written in WGSL, which is a shading language for WebGPU.

The shaders are delivered under the MIT License when copied from LLaMA.cpp, and any modifications made to them are also licensed under the MIT License.
